Preparation


There are several things/ingredients that you will need to scramble an egg. Among them are a good pan, I prefer non-stick, a rubber spatula (if you are using a non-stick pan), and all of your ingredients. Using a metal spatula on a non-stick pan can ruin the pan and make the non-stick surface significantly less efective. As a general rule for ingredients, simpler is better. Eggs are pretty simple: don't too much! All you need is a few good ingredients and practice; a great comprehensive list is outlined in the link above!


Execution


There are countless reciepes on the internet regarding how to scramble an egg, but this one will give you some of the best results. Admittedly, scrambling an egg is a pretty simple task, but little, nuanced changes can lead to an even better outcome. Some of these little things include: not adding seasoning too early and adding other additions in an unusual order. Again, as simple as eggs are, there's almost a learning curve to it. Making these little changes can bring your breakfast from okay to great!


Enjoyment!


You can always eat scrambled eggs plain, but eating something with them can make it even better. There are so many great options: from adding vegetables to the eggs to making other dishes to go alongside. Eggs are the cornerstone of any great breakfast, so long as you know what you're doing!
